====================================================

PROJECT NAME

Echoes of Impact

Tagline:
"Every donation has a story. Experience the lives you've changed."

====================================================

PROBLEM STATEMENT

People donate once and forget because they never experience the stories behind the impact.

Traditional donation platforms only show numbers like:

₹50,000 raised
120 beneficiaries
30 schools built

These statistics fail to create emotional connection.

People lose engagement because they never witness the human journey behind their contribution.

The goal is to transform donations into living stories through timelines, journals, updates, milestones and immersive storytelling.

The platform should make every donor emotionally invested in the impact they create.

FINAL GOAL

The experience should make users cry, smile, feel hope, and return every month—not because they are reminded to donate, but because they genuinely care about the people whose lives they're following.

The app should feel like a premium storytelling platform where donations become unforgettable human journeys rather than transactions.

Design every screen as if it is being judged by Apple Design Awards, Google Material Design Awards, and a global UX jury. Prioritize emotional storytelling, trust, accessibility, simplicity, and delightful interactions while maintaining pixel-perfect consistency.
